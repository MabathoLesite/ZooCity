GORILLA

Gorillas are the largest living primates and are native to the forests of central Africa. They are ground-dwelling and primarily herbivorous, feeding on leaves, stems, and fruit. Gorillas are social animals, living in groups led by a dominant male known as a silverback.

These animals are known for their intelligence and strong familial bonds. They communicate through a variety of vocalizations, gestures, and facial expressions. Gorillas are also capable of using tools, such as using sticks to extract termites from mounds.

Gorillas are critically endangered due to habitat loss, poaching, and disease. Conservation efforts are underway to protect their remaining populations and habitats.

COFFEE SHOP

The Coffee Shop is a cozy spot within the zoo where visitors can relax and enjoy a variety of beverages and snacks. It offers a selection of coffee, tea, pastries, and light meals, all made with high-quality ingredients.

With its comfortable seating and inviting atmosphere, the Coffee Shop is the perfect place to take a break during your zoo visit. Guests can enjoy their refreshments while overlooking the beautiful gardens and animal exhibits.

The Coffee Shop is committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly practices and sourcing ingredients locally whenever possible.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find the Coffee Shop near the entrance of the zoo, easily accessible from all main pathways.

GARDEN AMPHITHEATER

The Garden Amphitheater is an outdoor venue located within the zoo, designed for educational presentations and animal demonstrations. It features tiered seating for visitors and a stage area where zookeepers can interact with animals.

This amphitheater is surrounded by lush gardens, providing a natural and serene backdrop for performances. It is an ideal location for learning about wildlife conservation and the importance of protecting natural habitats.

Visitors can enjoy scheduled shows throughout the day, featuring a variety of animals and informative talks by knowledgeable staff.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find the Garden Amphitheater near the entrance of the zoo, easily accessible from all main pathways.

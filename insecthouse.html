INSECT HOUSE

The Insect House is a fascinating exhibit that showcases a variety of insect species from around the world. Visitors can observe these incredible creatures up close and learn about their unique behaviors and habitats.

With interactive displays and informative signage, the Insect House provides an engaging experience for guests of all ages. Highlights include live ant colonies, colorful butterflies, and exotic beetles.

The exhibit emphasizes the importance of insects in our ecosystem and promotes conservation efforts to protect these vital creatures.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find the Insect House located near the entrance of the zoo, easily accessible from all main pathways.

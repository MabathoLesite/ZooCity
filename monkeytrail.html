MONKEY TRAIL

The Monkey Trail is an exciting exhibit that allows visitors to observe various species of monkeys in a naturalistic setting. This habitat is designed to mimic the monkeys' native environments, featuring climbing structures, lush vegetation, and water features.

Visitors can walk along elevated pathways that provide a unique vantage point for viewing the monkeys as they swing through the trees and interact with their surroundings.

The exhibit is home to several species of monkeys, each with its own distinct behaviors and social structures. Knowledgeable staff are on hand to provide information and answer questions about these fascinating animals.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find the Monkey Trail located in the eastern section of the zoo, easily accessible from all main pathways.

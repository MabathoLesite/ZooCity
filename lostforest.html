LOST FOREST

The Lost Forest is a unique habitat within the zoo, designed to mimic the conditions of a tropical rainforest. It features a diverse range of plant species, creating a lush and vibrant environment for various animals.

This habitat is home to many species of birds, insects, and small mammals, all of which play a crucial role in the ecosystem. The dense foliage provides shelter and food for these animals, while also helping to maintain humidity levels within the forest.

Visitors can explore the Lost Forest through a network of winding paths and elevated walkways, allowing for an immersive experience in this enchanting environment.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find the Lost Forest located in the central area of the zoo, easily accessible from all main pathways.

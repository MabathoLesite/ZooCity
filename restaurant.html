OUTDOOR RESTAURANT

The Outdoor Restaurant offers a unique dining experience amidst the natural beauty of the zoo. Guests can enjoy a variety of dishes made from fresh, locally sourced ingredients while taking in views of the surrounding gardens and animal exhibits.

With both indoor and outdoor seating options, the restaurant provides a comfortable and inviting atmosphere for visitors to relax and recharge during their zoo adventure.

The menu features a range of options, including vegetarian and vegan dishes, ensuring that there is something for everyone to enjoy.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find the Outdoor Restaurant located near the entrance of the zoo, easily accessible from all main pathways.

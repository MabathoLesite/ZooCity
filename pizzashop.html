MOS PIZZA

Mos Pizza is a popular dining spot within the zoo, offering a variety of delicious pizzas made with fresh ingredients. The menu features classic favorites as well as unique creations inspired by global flavors.

With its casual atmosphere and family-friendly seating, Mos Pizza is the perfect place to take a break and refuel during your zoo adventure. Guests can enjoy their meals while overlooking the beautiful gardens and animal exhibits.

The restaurant is committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly practices and sourcing ingredients locally whenever possible.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find Mos Pizza near the entrance of the zoo, easily accessible from all main pathways.
